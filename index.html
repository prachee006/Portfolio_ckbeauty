About us

CK Beauty is a professional freelance makeup team based in Detroit, Michigan. We are made up of a team of professionals from all walks of life. We travel on-location all over Detroit.We specialize in several types of events. Are you planning your wedding and need a glam squad? Are you looking for a makeup artist for a photo-shoot? Does your daughter need her hair and makeup done for prom? Are you filming a commercial and need someone on set to prep the talent? CK Beauty is a one stop shop for all your makeup needs.
